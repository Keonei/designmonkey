Hello. I am a Pacific Northwest urban dwelling moss-footed tinker of things. I find serenity in the mountains, my headphones are full of metal, I tinker, I create, I wonder and sometimes I even pull out my easel and create worlds.

I obtained a Graphic Design degree in a pre-web world so in the years post graduation and with the changing industry I had to adapt. I am a self taught web designer who eventually obtained a certification to better my knowledge as a web developer.

This website is not only a showcase of work but a piece of work in itself. Over the years there have been many iterations of this online portfolio with specific challenges to overcome.
